# Bill Of Materials

## Printed Parts

- PETG filament
- top frame ring
- bottom frame ring
- corner blocks
- removable module trays
- side panels
- rear service spine
- power bus placeholder panel
- bottom intake fan panel
- top exhaust fan panel
- Mini PC airflow duct

## Hardware

- 4x M5 threaded rods
- M5 nuts and washers
- 4x metal guide rails, 10 x 3 mm flat bar placeholder
- M3 screws
- M3 screws for rail mounting
- M4 screws where higher panel strength is needed
- brass heat-set inserts
- rubber feet
- Velcro straps or soft elastic retention straps

## Cooling

- 2x 120 mm fans
- 120 mm dust filter for intake
- fan screws or vibration mounts

## Power And Electrical Placeholders

- external certified AC/DC power brick
- DC UPS board
- LiFePO4 battery pack or placeholder pack
- BMS
- DC-DC converters for 19 V / 12 V / 5 V
- fuse block
- terminal blocks
- XT30 connector
- JST-VH or Molex MicroFit connector
- short USB-C cable for Kingston XS2000

Exact electrical parts must be selected and checked before final mechanical dimensions are locked.
