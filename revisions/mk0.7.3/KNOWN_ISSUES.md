# mk0.7.3 Known Issues

- Real Mini PC, MikroTik, UPS, SSD bay, and battery dimensions are not yet locked.
- Power connectors, fuse holders, switch, and DC input connector are placeholder envelopes.
- Mini PC duct is not validated against the actual device intake and exhaust.
- Cable routing is a rough service-envelope model, not final wire harness engineering.
- Thermal behavior is not validated by CFD, smoke testing, or physical measurement.
- Full product readiness is deferred to mk1.0.
- Side-panel mount rails are rough mockup interfaces, not final shear-transfer engineering.
- Rail end mounts and tray support ledges are rough printable interfaces and still need physical fit testing against selected metal rails.
- Split-joint tabs are first-pass alignment/fastening features and need test coupons before printing full spine sections.
- Bottom filter retainer still needs fan-on retention testing.
