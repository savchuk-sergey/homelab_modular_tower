# mk0.7.3 Decisions

## D-001 Rough Assembly Before Final Hardware

mk0.7.3 prioritizes a mechanically buildable rough mockup over final hardware integration. Real measured components will be handled in mk1.0.

## D-002 Placeholder Honesty

Power, UPS, connector, and duct geometry remains placeholder-level, but must be named and documented as interface envelopes, not as final production geometry.

## D-003 Split Tall Parts

Oversized rear-service and power-bus parts are split into printable segments with alignment and fastening features. Diagonal printing is not accepted as a production workaround.

## D-004 Rough Mounting Rails Instead Of Final Shear Architecture

mk0.7.3 adds sectional side-panel mounting rails to provide a physical screw target for rough assembly. This is not the final shear-panel architecture. mk1.0 may replace these rails with a cleaner frame-integrated side-panel interface after real component dimensions are locked.

## D-005 Full-Height Spine And Power Parts Become Review References

The full-height rear-service and power-bus geometries remain useful as assembly/reference geometry, but they are not exported as printable plastic parts. Printable exports use split lower/upper sections.
