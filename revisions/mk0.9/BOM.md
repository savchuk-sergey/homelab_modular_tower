# mk0.9 BOM

## Printed

- PETG printed parts: base module, bottom grill, foot mounts, RPi/SSD module, mini PC placeholder module, roof module, dust filter slot, top filter slot, M5 rod caps.
- TPU printed parts: replaceable feet/dampers.

## Hardware

- M5 threaded rods, 4 pcs.
- M5 nuts and washers.
- M3/M4 screws for local module fastening.
- Heat-set inserts if local interface screws are promoted from clearance holes.
- 120 mm fan, 2 pcs.
- Dust filter material for bottom intake.
- Top guard/filter mesh.

## Devices / References

- Raspberry Pi 3B.
- External SSD.
- Mini PC placeholder.

Optional rubber feet can replace printed TPU feet during early fit checks.
