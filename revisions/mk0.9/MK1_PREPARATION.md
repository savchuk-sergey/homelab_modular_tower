# mk1.0 Preparation

Measure the real mini PC before starting mk1.0:

- Width.
- Depth.
- Height.
- Weight.
- Power button position.
- Power connector position.
- Ethernet, USB and HDMI port positions.
- Intake vent positions.
- Exhaust vent positions.
- Rubber feet locations.
- Screw holes, if any.
- Cable bend radius behind all ports.
- Required clearance around vents.
- Preferred extraction direction.
- Required access to the power button.

## mk0.9 Areas To Replace Or Refine

- `mini_pc_placeholder_module`.
- Mini PC tray.
- Mini PC retainer.
- Airflow guide.
- Airflow duct.
- Rear cable exit.
- Rear I/O cutout.
- Mini PC module height.
- Service access and future rear service spine mounting.
