# Known Issues For mk0.1

mk0.1 is an engineering checkpoint, not a final printable product.

Known issues:

- Device dimensions are placeholders.
- Real assembly has not been checked.
- Printability has not been physically checked.
- Module removal kinematics have not been verified.
- Real clearances have not been verified.
- Power bus is a placeholder.
- Final cable management is missing.
- Tray locks/latches are missing.
- Full ergonomic tray handles are missing.
- Side panels need lightening and structural optimization.
- Airflow needs optimization and thermal validation.
- Load on metal rails has not been checked.
- M5 rod fit has not been checked.
- All fastener holes need verification against real screws, inserts, washers, and nuts.
- Metal guide rail profile is only a 10 x 3 mm placeholder.
- Washer pockets and nut pockets need real hardware validation.
- Rear connector cutouts are placeholders.
- Cable bend radius and strain relief are not modeled.
- No collision study has been performed in a CAD assembly environment.
- No tolerance strategy exists yet for FDM shrink, PETG deformation, or insert installation.
