# Review Notes For mk0.1

## Positive Findings

- The model reads clearly as a mini-blade tower.
- M5 rods are present as visible assembly components.
- Metal guide rails are present as visible assembly components.
- Rear service spine is present.
- The design has a recognizable modular architecture.
- The construction looks significantly stronger than the first visual-only version.
- UPS, logic modules, network module, and Mini PC are separated into serviceable trays.
- Bottom intake, top exhaust, side perforation, and Mini PC duct are represented.

## Negative Findings

- There are no tray locks or service latches.
- Cable management is not complete.
- The top panel requires further engineering work.
- Ventilation needs optimization.
- Real device dimensions have not been transferred into the CAD model.
- Rail loading has not been calculated or tested.
- The rear service spine still needs connector-specific geometry.
- The power bus is not a real electrical/mechanical implementation.
- Tray extraction needs collision and hand-clearance checks.

## Freeze Decision

mk0.1 is accepted as the first engineering revision because the main architecture is visible and exportable. Remaining issues are deferred to mk0.2 and later revisions.
