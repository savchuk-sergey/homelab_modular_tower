# mk0.9.3 Known Limitations

- No physical fit-test has been performed.
- No slicer validation has been performed.
- No strength or stiffness test has been performed.
- No heat-set insert pull-out test has been performed.
- No CFD has been performed.
- Rail/shoe system remains experimental.
- Mini PC dimensions are still unknown and placeholder-based.
- Power distribution, router module, UPS module, and full rear service spine integration are out of scope.
- CadQuery mass targets must be treated as design intent only; real PETG mass must be checked in slicer.
- Current mesh-only estimates still exceed the desired mass ranges: `base_module` is about 249.7 g PETG and `roof_module` is about 245.9 g PETG.
