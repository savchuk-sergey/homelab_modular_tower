# mk0.9.3 BOM

## Printed

- PETG for base, roof, module shells, carriages, rail clips, and structural plastic subparts.
- TPU or rubber for replaceable feet.

## Hardware

- M5 threaded rods.
- M5 nuts and washers.
- M3 screws.
- M3 heat-set inserts.
- Aluminum U-channel rail `15 x 10 x 10 x 2 mm`.
- POM-C round rod `8 mm`, cut into `12 mm` shoes.
- 120 mm fans x2.
- Dust filter material.

## Reference Devices

- Raspberry Pi 3B.
- External SSD.
- Mini PC placeholder; real dimensions are not validated in this revision.
