# mk0.9.3

mk0.9.3 is a corrective CAD revision after mk0.9.2. Its purpose is to restore the CadQuery source as the reliable source of truth, make the project buildable/exportable from clean `master`, and make the active rail/carriage standard explicit.

This revision standardizes the side rail subsystem around aluminum U-channel rail `15 x 10 x 10 x 2 mm` and replaceable POM-C round shoes `8 x 12 mm`. PETG carries structure and socket geometry; it is not treated as the final sliding surface.

mk0.9.3 is not final print approval. It is buildable and exportable input for the next engineering review, slicer validation, and targeted fit tests.
