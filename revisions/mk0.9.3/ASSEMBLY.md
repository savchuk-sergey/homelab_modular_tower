# mk0.9.3 Assembly Notes

1. Install M5 threaded rods through the base and roof corner zones.
2. Fit the 120 mm intake fan, bottom grill, and dust filter slot into the base module.
3. Fit the 120 mm exhaust fan, top grill, top filter slot, and shroud into the roof module.
4. Install aluminum U-channel rails into the module rail pockets.
5. Insert POM-C shoes into PETG carriage sockets.
6. Use M3 clamp screws and heat-set inserts to retain or stop POM-C shoes. Do not thread screws directly into POM-C.
7. Slide the RPi + SSD carriage and mini PC placeholder carriage into the rail system.
8. Keep cable routing toward the rear service spine area; full power/router/UPS service integration remains out of scope for mk0.9.3.
