# mk0.9.3 to mk1 Preparation

Before mk1, validate the rail/shoe concept with coupon prints and real hardware:

- print a carriage side shoe socket coupon;
- test POM-C `8 x 12 mm` shoe insertion, retention, and replacement;
- test M3 clamp screw and heat-set insert pull-out resistance;
- check sliding behavior in real aluminum U-channel;
- measure the actual mini PC and replace placeholder dimensions;
- validate base and roof mass in slicer;
- verify fan/filter assembly clearance;
- review cable routing around the rear service spine before adding power/router/UPS modules.

mk0.9.3 should feed an engineering review, not a full-system print campaign.
