# mk0.5 Changelog

## Added

- Expanded `base_stability_plate` under the tower footprint.
- Wide replaceable TPU foot placeholders and PETG socket geometry.
- Structural mount tabs and 7 cover/mount points on the rear service spine.
- Stronger lower and upper side shear panel sections.
- Mini PC tray stop slot and screw/washer stop part.
- Optional torsion-brace mounting points on the rear spine.
- Review render targets:
  - `renders/review/stability_base.png`
  - `renders/review/torsion_frame.png`
  - `renders/review/mini_pc_service_position.png`

## Changed

- `CURRENT_REVISION` is now `mk0.5`.
- Assembly now places the expanded base before the bottom frame.
- Feet are positioned on the expanded base corners instead of directly under M5 rods.
- Export registry includes new mk0.5 parts.

## Not Changed

- Module order.
- Module slot standard.
- Power system architecture.
- Full airflow architecture.
- mk0.4 documentation.
