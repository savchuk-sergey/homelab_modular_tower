# mk0.5 Known Issues

- Rear spine printability as one long part is not physically validated.
- Real torsional stiffness still needs a hands-on twist test.
- TPU foot geometry is a placeholder until the real material hardness and screw retention are tested.
- Mini PC tray service travel is parametric but not tested with the real mini PC weight.
- Power bus component footprints remain placeholders.
- Airflow is preserved around the lower intake, but full thermal behavior is not solved in mk0.5.
- Torsion brace mounting points are prepared but the brace itself is not designed as a required part.
