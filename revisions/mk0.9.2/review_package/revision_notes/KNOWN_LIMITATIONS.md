# mk0.9.2 Known Limitations

- No physical print or fit test has been performed.
- No slicer print-time/material validation has been performed.
- No strength, stiffness, fatigue, or insert pull-out test has been performed.
- No CFD, thermal test, or fan pressure-drop analysis has been performed.
- Rail pocket/end-stop geometry is a corrective CAD implementation, not a
  proven final rail-retention design.
- Mini PC geometry remains placeholder-based.
- Power distribution, router, UPS, and full Rear Service Spine geometry remain
  out of scope.
- Long-thin printability review is still required for grill/filter/mount
  elements flagged by the analysis pipeline.
- The active modules exceed some mk0.9.1 weight targets in mesh estimate after
  rail pocket carrier integration; weight reduction should be reviewed before
  full print.
