# mk0.9.2 MK1 Preparation

## What mk0.9.2 enables

- A buildable and exportable rail/carriage subsystem for review.
- Printable rail receiving features in active payload module shells.
- Evidence package for external engineering review.
- A cleaner parameter set for future rail and carriage tuning.

## Required before mk1.0

- Measure the real Mini PC.
- Print tolerance coupons for POM-C shoe sockets and rail pockets.
- Confirm heat-set insert boss dimensions in PETG.
- Validate rail retention under service extraction loads.
- Check airflow with real fan/filter parts.
- Decide whether rail pocket carriers should be lightened or split into
  replaceable brackets.
- Revisit weight targets after slicer validation.

## Recommendation

Use `mk0.9.2` as a partial fit-test and review baseline, not as the final full
print baseline.
